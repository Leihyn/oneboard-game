# OneBoard Demo Script (3 minutes max)

## Before Recording

- Open https://oneboard-mauve.vercel.app in Chrome
- Have OneWallet extension ready and logged in
- Clear any previous game state (fresh page)
- Screen resolution: 1440x900 or higher
- Close all other tabs and notifications

## THE HOOK (0:00 to 0:15)

> "DeFi has dozens of protocols. OneDEX, staking, liquidity pools, RWA, prediction markets. For a new user, it is overwhelming. There is no fun way to learn an entire ecosystem without risking real money."

**[Do: Show the OneBoard landing page with the three AI opponent cards visible]**

## CONNECT AND START (0:15 to 0:35)

> "OneBoard changes that. It is a Monopoly style board game where every space is a real protocol in the OneChain ecosystem."

**[Do: Click "Connect Wallet" and connect OneWallet]**

> "When you hit Play Now, the game registers on chain immediately. One wallet signature. That is it."

**[Do: Click "Play Now". OneWallet popup appears. Sign the create_game transaction.]**

This is important. Let the wallet popup be visible on screen so judges see the on chain integration.

## GAMEPLAY (0:35 to 1:40)

> "You are playing against three AI opponents. Each one has a real personality powered by a large language model."

**[Do: Click "Roll Dice". Let the dice animate. Buy the property you land on.]**

> "The Degen Trader buys everything and trash talks in crypto slang. The Conservative Whale only moves when the math checks out. The MEV Bot calculates expected value on every single decision."

**[Do: End your turn. Let the AI opponents take their turns. Point to the Live Game Feed on the right as trash talk messages appear.]**

This is the money shot. Wait for the AI trash talk to appear in the chat panel. Let it sit on screen for a few seconds so judges can read it.

> "Watch the Live Game Feed. These are not preset lines. Every message is generated in real time by an LLM that knows the full game state."

**[Do: Play 2 to 3 more turns. Buy another property. Show the upgrade option if you get a full set. Click a player strip in the top bar to show their assets dropdown.]**

> "Own all properties in a category and you can upgrade. A basic OneDEX becomes an AMM, then a Hybrid, then a Full Orderbook. Rent scales with each level."

## AI PERSONALITIES (1:40 to 2:10)

> "The AI is not just playing the game. It is reacting to your moves, to other players, to the board state. Each personality responds differently to the same situation."

**[Do: Scroll the Live Game Feed to show contrasting messages from different AIs. Point out the visual differences: Degen in red italic, Whale in teal serif, MEV Bot in purple monospace.]**

> "The Degen says ape in. The Whale says wait for value. The MEV Bot gives you a probability score for why you are about to lose."

## WIN AND MINT NFT (2:10 to 2:30)

If you have not won yet, describe it:

> "When you bankrupt all three opponents, you win. And the game gives you something real."

**[Do: If possible, show the game over screen. Click "Mint Victory NFT". OneWallet popup appears. Sign.]**

> "One more wallet signature. A Victory NFT minted directly to your OneWallet. On chain proof that you dominated the board."

If you cannot get to the end screen in time, just describe it verbally over a screenshot.

## MULTIPLAYER AND CLOSE (2:30 to 3:00)

> "OneBoard also supports PvP multiplayer. Create a game, share the ID, and your friends join. Every action goes through Move smart contracts on OneChain."

**[Do: Quick flash of the /multiplayer page showing Create Game and Join Game sections]**

> "16 protocol spaces. 3 AI opponents with real personalities. LLM powered trash talk. On chain game creation. Victory NFTs. All built on OneChain with Move smart contracts."

**[Do: Show the landing page one more time]**

> "This is OneBoard."

## If Things Go Wrong

**OneWallet popup does not appear:** Skip the signing, say "the game also works in offline mode for testing" and continue playing.

**AI trash talk does not appear:** Say "the AI generates responses asynchronously" and keep playing. It will show up eventually.

**Game freezes:** Refresh the page and start over. You have 3 minutes so one restart is fine.

**You land on boring spaces:** Keep rolling. The gameplay will pick up. If needed, just narrate over it: "As you play more turns, the board fills up and rent starts flying."
